❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering, delivery, and returns.

Shipping & Delivery

How long does delivery take?
Orders are processed within 24-48 hours. Delivery takes 2-4 business days across Jamaica.

Do you deliver island-wide?
Yes! We partner with Tara and other couriers to deliver to all parishes in Jamaica.

Is there free delivery?
Free delivery on all orders over JMD 7,000.

Returns & Refunds

What is your return policy?
Due to the nature of our products, we do not offer refunds after delivery. However, we offer exchanges or store credit for damaged or incorrect items.

My package arrived damaged. What do I do?
Contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the damage. We'll arrange a replacement or store credit.

Customization

Can I customize my care package?
Absolutely! Choose our "Custom Bundle" option and pick the items that work best for you.

Can I add a gift note?
Yes! Just leave your message at checkout and we'll include a handwritten note.

Payments

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept credit cards, PayPal, bank transfers, and cash on delivery.

Is my payment secure?
Yes! Our checkout is encrypted and secure.
