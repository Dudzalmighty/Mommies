📦 Shipping & Returns Policy

Shipping Information

We partner with reliable courier services including Tara to ensure your care package arrives safely and on time.

  • Processing Time: Orders are processed within 24-48 hours of confirmation.
  • Delivery Time: 2-4 business days for Kingston and major towns, 3-5 business days for rural areas.
  • Shipping Cost: JMD 500 flat rate island-wide.
  • Free Shipping: On all orders over JMD 7,000.
  • Tracking: A tracking link will be sent via email once your order ships.

Returns & Exchanges

We want you to love your Momsies experience. If something isn't right, here's our policy:

  • No Refund Policy: Due to the nature of our products (personal care items), we do not offer refunds after delivery.
  • Damaged or Incorrect Items: If your package arrives damaged or contains incorrect items, contact us within 48 hours with photos. We will offer a replacement or store credit.
  • Exchanges: Store credit or exchanges are available within 14 days of delivery for unopened, unused items.
  • How to Request: Email hello@momsiesja.com with your order number and photos (if applicable).
